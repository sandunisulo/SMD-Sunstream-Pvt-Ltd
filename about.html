About Us

Driving Sustainable Energy for a Better Tomorrow

Our Vision

To be a leader in sustainable energy solutions in Sri Lanka, empowering communities and businesses through innovative and reliable solar energy systems that contribute to a cleaner, greener future.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, cost-effective solar energy solutions that reduce carbon footprints and promote energy independence, while maintaining exceptional customer service and environmental responsibility.

Company Profile

SMD Sunstream (Pvt) Ltd is a diversified Sri Lankan company specializing in renewable energy solutions and sustainable technologies. We provide solar PV systems for residential, commercial, and industrial clients including installation, commissioning, and long-term maintenance.

With a strong commitment to quality, safety, and long-term performance, our expert team delivers customized rooftop and ground-mounted solar solutions, hybrid systems, and energy storage systems.

In addition to renewable energy, we are involved in the distribution of people-care products that meet international standards while remaining affordable and suitable for local markets.
